Riding with Care & Confidenceركوب بعناية وثقة

At Legacy Equestrian, we believe every rider deserves the opportunity to experience the joy and confidence that comes with horsemanship. Our Special Needs program offers gentle, fully adaptive lessons tailored to each individual — nurturing physical coordination, emotional balance, and a deep, trusting bond with the horse.

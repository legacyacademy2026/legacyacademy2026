Special Needs Lessons

About This Program

Riding with Care & Confidence

At Legacy Equestrian, we believe every rider deserves the opportunity to experience the joy and confidence that comes with horsemanship. Our Special Needs program offers gentle, fully adaptive lessons tailored to each individual — nurturing physical coordination, emotional balance, and a deep, trusting bond with the horse. Every session is guided by a certified instructor in a calm, safe, and encouraging environment.

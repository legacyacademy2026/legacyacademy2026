Horse Training

At Legacy Equestrian, we offer professional horse training programs designed to unlock the full potential of every horse. Whether refining responsiveness, building discipline, or advancing performance, our certified trainers craft a plan tailored to each horse's temperament and stage.

We also specialize in breaking young and unhandled horses — using a gentle, patient approach that introduces each horse to the saddle and rider with care, building lasting confidence and trust from the very first session.

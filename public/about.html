About Us

Our Story

A Lifelong Love for the Horse

Legacy Equestrian was founded on a simple belief: that the bond between horse and rider transforms lives. Under the guidance of Coach Mervat, our academy blends disciplined, world-class instruction with genuine warmth and care for every rider who walks through our gates.

From first-time riders taking their very first hand-led steps to advanced competitors refining their craft, we meet each student where they are — nurturing confidence, skill, and a deep respect for these remarkable animals.
